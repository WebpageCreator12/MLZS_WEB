How to Enroll

Our Simple 5-Step Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit the online registration form and pay the application fee. You will receive a unique registration ID.

2

Interaction / Assessment

Schedule a meeting with the Head of Admissions or participate in an age-appropriate written assessment for the student.

3

Document Verification

The required documents (listed below) must be submitted to the Admissions office for verification and approval.

4

Enrollment Confirmation

Upon successful verification and approval, you will receive an official Offer of Admission via email.

Details & Criteria

Key Information for Prospective Parents

Required Documents Checklist

  • Completed and signed Admission Form.
  • Child's Original Birth Certificate (for verification).
  • Two recent passport-size photographs of the student.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school (Grade 2 onwards).
  • Copy of the student's latest academic progress report.
  • Aadhar/ID Proof of both parents and the student.
