A Message From Our Principal

Welcome to Mount Litera Zee School

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire."

At MLZS, we believe in creating an atmosphere where every child feels empowered to ask questions, explore their passions, and develop into a well-rounded individual. Our commitment extends beyond rote learning; we focus on critical thinking, ethical values, and fostering a global perspective.

Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and dynamic curriculum ensure that your child receives the very best start to their academic journey. We invite you to explore our campus and witness the learning environment firsthand.
